The Heroes Project - Resources


Patient Advocate Foundation A national organization providing patients with arbitration, mediation, and negotiation to settle issues with access to care, medical debt, and job retention related to their illness.
HealthWell Foundation A foundation dedicated to improving access to care for America's underinsured by providing financial assistance to eligible individuals.
Cancer Care Assistance Cancer Care offer limited financial assistance for cancer-related costs and co-pays, and professional oncology social workers can help you find additional resources.
Patient Access Network Offers support for underinsured patients, helping them access the medical treatments they need.
SamFund for Young Adults Provides financial assistance and support to young adults who are struggling financially due to cancer treatment.
CancerCare Co-Payment Assistance Foundation A foundation ensuring that cancer patients have access to care by offering co-payment assistance.
NeedyMeds A non-profit organization that provides information on programs that help people who can't afford medications and healthcare costs.
To Show We Care To We Show Care invites patients and caregivers in Boston's Metropolitan and North Shore areas to all-expenses-paid events which include a reception and tickets to a performing arts performance, a movie, or playing games at an arcade.
Cancer and Careers Empowers and educates people with cancer to thrive in their workplace by offering expert advice, interactive tools, and educational events.
Job Accommodation Network Provides guidance on workplace accommodations and disability employment issues, helping both employees and employers navigate the challenges.
CEW Foundation: Rebecca Nellis A charitable arm of the Cosmetic Executive Women community, focusing on philanthropic initiatives related to women's well-being, including support for cancer patients.
Annie Appleseed Advocates for holistic, natural approaches to health and cancer therapy, providing information on complementary treatments and organic lifestyles.
Hippocrates Health Institute A leader in natural and complementary healthcare, offering programs and therapies that focus on holistic wellness and disease prevention.
Block Cancer Center Pioneers in integrative cancer treatment, combining conventional and complementary therapies to address the whole person, not just the disease.
Road to Recovery (ACS) Sponsored by the American Cancer Society, they offer patients crucial transportation services to and from their cancer treatments.
Compass to Care An altruistic non-profit organization, they not only coordinate but also finance travel and accommodation, ensuring families with children suffering from cancer can reach the best doctors and treatments available.
Tommys Place A Massachusetts based nonprofit corporation that owns and operates year-round magical vacation homes for kids fighting cancer in Falmouth, MA and Centerville, MA on Cape Cod.
Christopher's Haven A Massachusetts based nonprofit corporation that owns and operates year-round magical vacation homes for kids fighting cancer in Falmouth, MA and Centerville, MA on Cape Cod.
